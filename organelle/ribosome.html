The ribosome is an organelle located in many places through the cell. It converts mRNA into proteins. Proteins are necessary for every cell for growth, repair, and survival.

Structure
Ribosomes are small structures made of ribosomal RNA and protein. It reads genetic code and assembles amino acids.
